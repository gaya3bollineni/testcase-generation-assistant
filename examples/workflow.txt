Actors:
- Customer
- Claims Processing System
- Claims Adjuster

Preconditions:
- Policy is active
- Customer is authenticated
- Claim has not been previously submitted

Workflow Steps:
1. Customer submits a claim with amount and supporting documents
2. System validates claim against policy coverage
3. System checks whether claim amount exceeds manual review threshold
4. If threshold is exceeded, claim is routed to a claims adjuster
5. Adjuster reviews claim and approves or rejects it
6. System updates claim status and notifies customer

Business Rules:
- Claims over the manual review threshold must not be auto-approved
- Duplicate claims must be flagged for investigation
- Claim payout must not exceed remaining policy coverage


Constraints:
- Regulatory audit logging is required for all claim decisions
- Claim processing must complete within defined SLA
- Payout actions are irreversible once executed
